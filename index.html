HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

  • The Mind Map example shows Ideas, Categories, and Things which are all Categorical Attributes. They represent one of many abstract or concrete objects. The Marks are Points which represent each of the Mind Map's Attributes and Lines which represent the links between the Attributes. The Points Channel is the Color, indicating which type of Attribute each point is. The Lines Channel is Positions which indicates which Points are connected.

  • The Stacked Bar Chart example shows bars split into Categorical Attributes [A, B, C, D] and Categorical Attributes within the bars. There are Ordered Attributes that vertically measure the bars and their sections. The Marks are the Lines for each section of the bars. The Lines Channels are Color to show each of the four sections of the bar as a different category and Length which indicates the quantitative measure on the chart's y axis.
